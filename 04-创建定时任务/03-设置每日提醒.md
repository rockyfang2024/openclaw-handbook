# 4.3 设置每日提醒

**根据不同场景设置多个定时提醒**

---

## 💡 常见提醒场景

| 场景 | Cron 表达式 | 用途 |
|------|-------------|------|
| 早安提醒 | `0 7 * * *` | 每天 7:00 |
| 午间提醒 | `0 12 * * *` | 每天 12:00 |
| 晚间总结 | `0 22 * * *` | 每天 22:00 |
| 工作日提醒 | `0 9 * * 1-5` | 周一至周五 9:00 |
| 会议提醒 | `0 14 * * 3` | 每周三 14:00 |

---

## 📝 创建多提醒配置

在 `config.yaml` 中配置多个定时任务：

```yaml
cron:
  morning:
    script: "~/.openclaw/cron-scripts/morning-quote.py"
    schedule: "0 7 * * *"
    channel: feishu
    target: "你的飞书open_id"

  lunch:
    script: "~/.openclaw/cron-scripts/lunch-reminder.py"
    schedule: "0 12 * * *"
    channel: feishu
    target: "你的飞书open_id"

  evening:
    script: "~/.openclaw/cron-scripts/evening.py"
    schedule: "0 22 * * *"
    channel: feishu
    target: "你的飞书open_id"
```

---

## 🔄 重启生效

```bash
openclaw gateway restart
```

---

## ✅ 查看定时任务状态

```bash
openclaw cron list
```
